College Assistant Professor of Economics

Girton College, University of Cambridge

Collin M. Constantine is a College Assistant Professor of Economics and Official Fellow at Girton College, University of Cambridge, where he also serves as Director of Studies in Economics.

Constantine is an applied theorist working at the intersection of international macroeconomics, income inequality, and political economy. Specifically, he focuses on foreign exchange stock–flow dynamics, banking and international finance, and macroeconomic policy. He also explores the interplay between economic development, the distribution of income and wealth, and political conflict. His motivation comes from real-world puzzles that emerge when conventional theory fails to explain key stylized facts.
