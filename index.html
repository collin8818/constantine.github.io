About

I am Collin Constantine, College Assistant Professor of Economics at Girton College, University of Cambridge. My research lies at the intersection of international macroeconomics, trade, and political economy, with particular focus on balance-of-payments constraints, distributional conflict, and the macroeconomic implications of trade policy. I am motivated by questions of economic justice and the structural challenges faced by small open economies in the global system.
