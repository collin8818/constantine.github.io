Macro-Finance Intelligence is concerned with economies where standard models travel poorly: import-dependent economies, financially dollarised systems, commodity exporters, foreign-exchange-constrained states, and countries where fiscal dominance weakens monetary control.

MFI provides historically informed and institutionally grounded macro-financial analysis for clients who need to understand how policy choices, institutional constraints, and balance-sheet dynamics shape economic and political risk.

The purpose of MFI is to help decision-makers distinguish symptoms from causes, temporary relief from durable adjustment, and official narratives from underlying macro-financial realities.
