100-word biography and high-resolution headshots for press and event organisers.

Collin M. Constantine is a College Assistant Professor of Economics and Official Fellow at Girton College, University of Cambridge, where he also serves as Director of Studies in Economics. His research sits at the intersection of international macroeconomics, finance, and political economy, with particular attention to how fiscal dominance, exchange rate dynamics, and inequality shape development outcomes in small open and multi-ethnic economies. He is Co-Editor of Economics and serves on the Editorial Review Board of the Journal of Stratification Economics. Constantine engages policymakers and delivers invited talks at international conferences, academic seminars, and policy forums.
