Research Assistant Opportunities

I occasionally work with pre-docs and graduate students as research assistants on projects related to macro-finance, political economy, and development. Typical tasks may include literature reviews, data collection, coding, or help preparing presentation slides.

I welcome interest from students who are curious, motivated, and eager to build research skills. Opportunities are limited and depend on current projects, but I’m glad to hear about your interests.

If you would like to be considered, please send a brief note outlining your academic background, research interests, and relevant skills.
