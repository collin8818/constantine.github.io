Research Assistant Opportunities

I occasionally work with pre-docs and graduate students as research assistants on projects related to macro-finance, political economy, and development. Typical tasks may include literature reviews, data collection, coding, or assistance with preparing presentation slides.

I welcome interest from students who are curious, motivated, and eager to develop research skills. While opportunities are limited and depend on current projects, I am always glad to hear from students about their research interests and aspirations.

If you are interested in working with me, please send a brief note outlining your academic background, research interests, and relevant skills.
